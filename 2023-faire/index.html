Meet the Speakers

Jeremy Osborne

Jeremy Osborne fell into Frontend engineering around the iPhone 1, the death of Flash, and back when IE6 still commanded respect. Now he works with too many JavaScript and CSS frameworks. When he's not Ctrl-C-ing his infinite loops, he's likely spending time with his son, rock climbing, or baking yet another loaf of sourdough bread.

Luis Garcia

Luis Garcia is an expert in digital accessibility with over 10 years of experience. He's currently developing a free/open source tool to lower the barrier to entry for evaluating accessibility. He also actively contributes to WCAG 3, the next major revision to the accessibility guidelines, as well as other accessibility-related standards and volunteer work.

Stations

PORTFOLIO PEER REVIEW WITH JUSTINA

Justina will lead the portfolio peer review station where participants will pair off and give each other feedback on their portfolios. After a set amount of time, all the participants will rotate partners.

ICEBREAKER STARTER QUESTIONS WITH SEIKO

Seiko has a set of starter questions available to break the ice with your fellow participants. You are bound to have some interesting conversation in this activity.
